# Configuration

1. Ensure MapShare is enabled in the [Social](https://explore.garmin.com/Social) tab of your Garmin Explore account.
2. Take note of your unique MapShare Address (i.e. share.garmin.com/[your_identifier]). If you have set a password to protect your MapShare page, you will also need to take note of this.
3. Visit your MapShare address in your browser and verify that there is MapShare data (i.e. a message or track point) displaying. Any test message [shared with your MapShare page](https://support.garmin.com/?faq=p2lncMOzqh71P06VifrQE7) will work.
4. With this plugin activated, go to WP Admin > Settings > inReach MapShare.
5. Enter your MapShare address and submit.
6. You should see your most recent location, or multiple locations if you supplied a date range.
7. Add the provided Shortcode anywhere Shortcodes are supported to display the content on your site.
